I am the Henry Rutgers Term Chair in Data Science and Associate Professor of Mathematics and Computer Science at Rutgers Newark.

You can learn about the research done in my lab through the Cognitive and Data Sciences (CoDaS) lab website. It includes a rather silly, rather dated picture of me at Donkey Hill cutoff in New Hampshire, a list of the fantastic folks who are currently in or have moved on from my lab, as well as a proliferation of papers.

My work is interdisciplinary and I get excited about research that is both theoretical and relevant to real-world problems. Since I have moved back east, I go into the city roughly one day a week to chat with people in industry about the problems that they are excited about. Please reach out if you want to drink COFFEE! and chat.
